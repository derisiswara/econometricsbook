**log volatility return:** 
$$
\sigma^2_{it} = 0.361[ln(P^{max}_{i,t}) - ln(P^{min}_{i,t-1}))]^2
$$

$$
\sigma_{it} = 100*(\sqrt{252*\sigma^2_{it}})
$$
